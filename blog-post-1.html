Building a Real-Time Logger

May 10, 2025

In my final year project, I built Our Logger, a real-time logging tool to monitor application performance. The challenge was ensuring scalability under high traffic.

I used JavaScript and WebSocket for real-time updates, integrating it with a simple HTML/CSS dashboard. Optimizing the WebSocket connection reduced latency by 40%.

This project taught me the importance of balancing performance and usability in web applications, a skill I hope to apply in graduate research.
